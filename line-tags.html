Line Tags

DraftVerify Line Tags provide visible assurance that every non-alcoholic draft line is clearly marked, serialized, and verified.

Purpose

Proper identification of non-alcoholic lines is critical to prevent cross-contact and maintain beverage purity. DraftVerify Line Tags introduce a standardized color and serial code system for every certified installation.

Design & Function

1️⃣ Color-Coded by Category

Blue for beer, green for kombucha, and gold for nitro coffee — instantly recognizable for staff and inspectors.

2️⃣ Serialized Verification ID

Each tag carries a unique DraftVerify ID number linking the line to its verification record for traceability.

3️⃣ Durable & Hygienic

Made from food-safe polymer resistant to moisture, sanitizer, and temperature variation — built to last service life of the line.

Compliance & Audit Trail

Every verified venue receives a logbook referencing its line tag IDs. Auditors or distributors can confirm authenticity via QR or NFC tag scan, ensuring ongoing compliance under the DraftVerify Standard.
