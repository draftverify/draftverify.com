The Genuine NFC Tag

One Tag. One Scan. Zero Ambiguity.

The V2 DraftVerify Line Tag is both a physical identifier and a digital trust mark. Behind the clear NON-ALCOHOLIC visuals is a cryptographically-unique NFC ID registered in our system — impossible to duplicate, impossible to fake.

  • Unique NFC ID: every tag is pre-registered to a brewery, SKU, and batch sequence.
  • Cloud-linked: a scan retrieves live product data and authenticity through the DraftVerify API.
  • Holographic-verified: matches your faucet’s holographic tower sticker for end-to-end identification.
  • Anti-tamper design: damage, removal, or mismatch is detected instantly during scanning.

FAQ

Common Questions

Is the NFC data editable?

No. Tag IDs are locked, registered, and cannot be duplicated or rewritten by end-users.

Do tags require an app?

No app required — any NFC-enabled smartphone can trigger a verification check instantly.

Do we need physical tags if we can scan?

Yes — the physical + digital pairing is what creates the global standard for NA draft identification.

What if someone removes a tag?

Removed, damaged, or missing tags are flagged immediately during scanning and can be reissued.
