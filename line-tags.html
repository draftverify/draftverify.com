The Genuine NFC Tag

One Tag. One Scan. Zero Ambiguity.

A DraftVerify line tag is both a physical identifier and a digital trust mark. Beneath the clear NON-ALCOHOLIC visuals is a cryptographically-unique NFC ID registered in our system — impossible to duplicate and impossible to fake.

  • Unique NFC ID: every tag is pre-registered to a brewery, SKU, and batch sequence.
  • Cloud-connected: scanning retrieves live product data and authenticity through the DraftVerify Registry.
  • Hologram-matched: pairs with the tower’s holographic seal for end-to-end identification.
  • Anti-tamper: removal or damage is immediately flagged during verification.

FAQ

Common Questions

Is the NFC data editable?

No. NFC IDs are locked, registered, and cannot be duplicated or rewritten by end-users.

Do tags require an app?

No app required — any NFC-enabled smartphone can verify authenticity immediately.

Do we still need physical tags if scanning works?

Yes — the visual + digital pairing is what creates the global standard for NA draft identification.

What if a tag is removed or damaged?

Removed or compromised tags are flagged during scanning and can be instantly reissued.
