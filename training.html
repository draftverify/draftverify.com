Training & Certification

Empowering every operator to uphold verified standards for non-alcoholic draft systems — through concise, practical learning.

Overview

The DraftVerify Training Program provides a 10-minute digital module that teaches identification, verification, and sanitation best practices.

Course Structure

Module 1 — Identification

How to visually confirm and label non-alcoholic lines using line tags and tower stickers.

Module 2 — Verification

Understanding compliance logs, serial codes, and how to perform quick line checks during service.

Module 3 — Sanitation

Reviewing cleaning schedules, approved materials, and safety standards to prevent cross-contact.

Certification

Upon completion, each participant receives a digital certificate valid for 12 months. Venues maintaining full staff compliance retain verified status in the DraftVerify directory.
