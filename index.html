The Global Standard

Setting the Worldwide Standard for Non-Alcoholic Draft Systems

DraftVerify defines the benchmark for safety, consistency, and integrity in non-alcoholic draft service. Our certification framework ensures every pour—from brewery to bar—is verified, separated, and trusted.

The Three Pillars of Verification

  • Line Tags: Permanent identification on every dedicated non-alcoholic draft line, creating traceable separation from alcoholic products.
  • Tower Stickers: Visual confirmation for staff and inspectors that the tap meets DraftVerify standards for clean, compliant service.
  • Training: A concise certification module that ensures every team member understands correct line management, cleaning, and verification practices.

Why the Standard Exists

As non-alcoholic beer and cocktails enter mainstream draft systems, consistency and safety demand oversight. DraftVerify prevents cross-contamination and protects brand credibility through a single, auditable framework recognized across breweries, venues, and jurisdictions.

Our standard bridges the gap between engineering precision and hospitality practicality—built for the brewer, enforced by the operator, trusted by the consumer.

Global Recognition & Compliance

DraftVerify is the only international verification system designed exclusively for non-alcoholic draft. Certification under v1.0 aligns venues with best-in-class sanitation, labeling, and operational protocols that scale globally while respecting local regulations.
