Why DraftVerify Exists

As non-alcoholic beer and cocktails grow on draft, shared infrastructure introduces risk — from cross-contact to misidentification. DraftVerify provides a unified verification framework, ensuring separation, documentation, and consumer trust in every pour.

Line Tags

Permanent identification on every NA draft line, establishing clear traceability and system separation.

Tower Stickers

Visual verification that taps meet DraftVerify guidelines for hygiene, labeling, and compliance.

Training

Concise modules aligning operators, breweries, and regulators on responsible NA draft management.
