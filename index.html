Separation & Couplers

Hardware separation that can’t be “accidentally” bypassed

Unique NA line identification, coupler policies, and bar-side controls that prevent cross-contact with alcoholic lines during changeovers and service.

Line Integrity

Cleaning, purging, and verification intervals

Defined intervals, test procedures, and record-keeping to maintain sensory integrity and eliminate residual alcohol or flavor carry-over.

Labeling & Audit

On-system labels + audit trail

On-tap labeling, QR/NFC compatibility, and a minimal audit log so operators and auditors can validate compliance in seconds.

Commissioning

Commissioning checklist for new NA lines

From keg bay to faucet: verified parts, sanitized pathways, purged lines, and sign-off criteria to declare an NA line “in-service.”

Operations

Simple rules — strong outcomes

Clear responsibilities for brewery, distributor, and venue; daily visual checks that take under a minute; escalation paths when issues arise.
