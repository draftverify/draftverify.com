The Global Standard

Setting the Worldwide Standard for Non-Alcoholic Draft Systems

DraftVerify defines the benchmark for safety, consistency, and integrity in non-alcoholic draft service. Our framework verifies separation, documentation, and operational alignment — promoting transparency and confidence throughout the supply chain.

How Verification Works

  • Line Tags: Permanent identification for every dedicated NA line, creating traceable separation.
  • Tower Stickers: Visual confirmation for staff and inspectors that taps meet DraftVerify guidelines.
  • Training: Concise modules that align teams on correct line management, cleaning, and documentation.

DraftVerify is an independent verification framework. It provides voluntary process and documentation standards related to non-alcoholic draft system practices. DraftVerify does not guarantee product purity, safety, or compliance with regulatory or legal requirements. Users are responsible for maintaining their own operational and hygiene standards.
