The Global Standard

Setting the Worldwide Standard for Non-Alcoholic Draft Systems

DraftVerify defines the benchmark for safety, consistency, and integrity in non-alcoholic draft service. Our framework verifies separation, documentation, and operational alignment — promoting transparency and confidence throughout the supply chain.

The Three Pillars of Verification

  • Line Tags: Permanent identification on every dedicated non-alcoholic draft line, creating traceable separation from alcoholic products.
  • Tower Stickers: Visual confirmation for staff and inspectors that the tap meets DraftVerify guidelines for clean, compliant service.
  • Training: A concise educational module that helps teams understand correct line management, cleaning, and verification practices.

Why the Framework Exists

As non-alcoholic beer and cocktails enter mainstream draft systems, consistency and safety demand oversight. DraftVerify addresses cross-contamination and labeling concerns through a single, auditable framework recognized across breweries, venues, and jurisdictions.

Our system bridges the gap between engineering precision and hospitality practicality—built for the brewer, supported by the operator, and trusted by the consumer.

Alignment & Recognition

DraftVerify aligns with the hygiene objectives of DIN 6650-6 while extending those principles to non-alcoholic system separation and transparency. It provides a common language for responsible draft management worldwide.

Adoption & Support
