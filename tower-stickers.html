Pillar: Tower Stickers

Tower Stickers

High-visibility markings for NA taps at the point of dispense to guide staff and guests in real time.

Purpose

On-tower clarity

Immediate differentiation of NA taps reduces error risk during busy service.

Placement

Where to apply

Apply at eye-line on tower face or directly adjacent to faucet handles per the Standard.

Design

Consistent look

Use the approved NA indicator design and size for universal recognition across venues.

Maintenance

Keep visible

Replace if worn, obstructed, or adhesive fails; visibility is mandatory for compliance.

Reference

See the Standard

Specifications are detailed in DraftVerify Standard v1.0.
