Tower Stickers

DraftVerify Tower Stickers provide a clear, standardized visual cue on every draft tower, signaling verified non-alcoholic service.

Purpose

Tower Stickers are the first thing guests and staff see — a visible promise of safety and quality. They distinguish verified non-alcoholic draft lines from alcoholic ones, eliminating confusion and risk.

Design & Materials

1️⃣ Color-Coded Verification

Each sticker carries the same verified color code as its line tag, aligning visual standards across the system.

2️⃣ Durable Adhesive

Made from temperature-resistant vinyl with a waterproof adhesive that withstands cleaning and condensation.

3️⃣ Transparent Identification

Each tower sticker includes the DraftVerify mark and certification date for quick inspection and peace of mind.

Placement & Compliance

Stickers are applied to the tower base, near the faucet, or any visible surface facing staff and guests. Verification officers record each placement as part of the venue’s compliance log.
