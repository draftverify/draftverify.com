Why DraftVerify Is Priced This Way

DraftVerify protects breweries, distributors, and venues from the most overlooked failure mode in the beverage industry: misidentification of non-alcoholic draft at the keg, coupler, or faucet.

A single mis-serve event can result in:

  • liability exposure and guest complaints
  • insurance or legal documentation requirements
  • brand damage for both brewery and venue
  • distributor–venue finger-pointing
  • loss of trust in NA draft as a category

The DraftVerify Standard provides a unified physical and digital identity system — keg tags, NFC verification, and traceability records — ensuring NA draft lines are clearly separated, auditable, and defensible across the entire draft pathway from keg → coupler → line → faucet.

Pricing reflects the operational value and risk reduction delivered at each scale, from a single taproom to national and global draft programs. As adoption grows, pricing may evolve to reflect expanded infrastructure and services.
