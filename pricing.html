Frequently Asked Questions

How long does verification take?

Most venues complete self-verification within 7–10 business days. Multi-site programs may take longer depending on internal training rollout.

Is there an annual renewal requirement?

Yes. Certificates remain valid for 12 months. Renewal ensures adherence to updates in hygiene practices and system-separation guidelines.

Can we customize training?

Enterprise programs can integrate internal SOPs, brand policies, and venue-specific procedures into the DraftVerify training portal.

What if our venue adds more taps later?

You can order additional kits at any time and provision more training seats. Your certificate can be updated after re-verification.
