FAQ

Do these prices include the physical NFC tags and decals?

Yes. Each plan includes the NFC assets, decals, and setup guides required to bring your NA draft program into alignment with the DraftVerify standard. Larger tiers include more tags and replenishment options.

Can we start with a pilot before rolling out across all venues?

Absolutely. Many partners start with one flagship venue or a small group, then expand once internal teams and distributors are comfortable with the workflow.

Do you support both non-alcoholic and alcoholic draft lines?

DraftVerify is built around non-alcoholic risk first, but the registry and tagging model can extend to alcoholic SKUs where financial, brand, or regulatory risk justify tighter controls.

What if our footprint doesn’t fit neatly into one tier?

That’s common. Use the onboarding form to outline your breweries, distributors, and venues and we’ll scope a plan that matches your real draft footprint.

Not sure where to slot your draft footprint? Send us your venues and distributors and we’ll build a simple, phased rollout plan around your reality.
